ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE IN ACADEMIA: FUTURE PROSPECTIVE


The academic processes are becoming more demanding and time- consuming than ever before. This is due to the emphasis on teaching effectiveness and quality assurance. In this seminar, we will discuss some of the advanced Artificial Intelligence solutions which could be implemented to speed up the academic process, makes it more efficient, and above all save time and effort..

Speaker: Prof. Nazar Zaki

Nazar Zaki is a Professor of Computer Science (AI and Machine-Learning). He served as a Chair, Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering, CIT, United Arab Emirates for almost 10 years introduced new academic programs and contributed significantly to the establishment and success of the department. Dr. Zaki is a founder and the Director of the Big Data Analytics Center. His research interest is in the fields of data mining, machine learning, and bioinformatics. He mainly focuses on developing intelligent algorithms to solve problems in domains such as healthcare and education. He published more than 120 scientific results in reputable journals and conferences. Dr. Zaki is currently an Associate Editor of Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence (IF 6.212), and IEEE Access (IF 3.367).
