Dr. Fida Dankar

College of Information Technology (CIT)

Fida Dankar is an Associate Professor at the College of Information Technology at UAEU. She holds a PhD in Computer Science and a Master in Mathematics from University of Ottawa in Canada. Prior to joining UAEU, Fida was a research Scientist at IBM Canada Research and Development Centre, where she worked on the secure and private mining of distributed health data.

Fida did her Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Electronic Health Information Lab in the Children's Hospital of Eastern Ontario; her postdoctoral work was devoted to facilitating the sharing of health information for secondary purposes while protecting the privacy of patients and the identity of providers. Currently, her research focuses on the development of multidisciplinary approaches for the private and secure sharing/mining of health data that uses methods from cryptography, biomedical knowledge modeling and policy analysis.
