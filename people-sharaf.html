The Big Data Analytics Center (BIDAC)


Dr. Mohamed Sharaf

College of Information Technology (CIT)

Mohamed Sharaf is an Associate Professor in Computer Science at the United Arab Emirates University (UAEU), which he joined in 2019. Prior to that, he held positions as a Senior Lecturer at the University of Queensland, and a Research Fellow at the University of Toronto. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Pittsburgh in 2007. His research interest lies in the general area of Data Science, with a special emphasize on large-scale big data analytics, interactive human-in-the-loop data exploration, and scalable data visualization. His research contributions have been published in around 65 conference papers and journal articles including top-ranked journal articles (e.g., TODS, PVLDB, VLDB, TKDE, DAPD, WWW, Inf. Syst. journals) and top-ranked conferences papers (e.g., SIGMOD, VLDB, ICDE, CIKM, SSDBM, ICDM). He served on the program committees of all the major data management conferences including SIGMOD, VLDB, ICDE, EDBT, and CIKM. He also served as the general chair of MobiDE’13, the program committee chair of MobiDE’12, SMDB’14, ADC’14, ADC’15, ExploreDB’17, and an area chair for ICDE’18.
